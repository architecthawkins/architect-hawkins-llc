Building D: 25,915 SF - Veterinary Sciences + STEM Labs
This building consists of surgery labs, animal holding rooms, STEM project labs and classrooms, as well outdoor kennels, dog runs, and an open corral for large animals.
Building G: 29,900 SF – Cosmetology & Dental Assisting
The cosmetology program is designed as a working salon for students and works in partnership with the community to provide cosmetology services for the benefit of all. The Dental Assisting program is designed with an open lab concept, equipped with innovating dental equipment to provide excellent working environments for students.
Phase 3B: New Build
Building F: 36,432 SF - Health Sciences
This open floor plan concept for simulated health clinic allows students to work in different areas based on instruction, where supervision and accessibility to equipment is inherent in all aspects the design.
Phase 4: New Build
Building E: 26,454 SF - Welding & Auto Technology
This building houses high bay labs with indoor and outdoor project spaces for project based learning. Classrooms and instructor spaces are adjacent for accessibility and flexibility of learning opportunities for both welding and auto technology programs.
