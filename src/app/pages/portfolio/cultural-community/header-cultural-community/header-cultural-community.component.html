Cultural / Community
These are the spaces for enlighten of the mind and senses. It is the in gathering of diverse people together – places that celebrate life. The libraries, hospitals, museums, senior centers – all these touch a core of the imparting of knowledge, the celebration of the past, the caring and socialization of the elderly, the caring for life – and those that have come before. I have worked on these facilities that open the senses to see the simplicity of life – to have a gentle breeze caress your face, to understand the importance of having a hand to hold for comfort, and understand that the best things in life are given and not received.

Tohono O’odham Nation: Museum / Cultural Center       Topawa, AZ
35,000 SF Museum
5,000 SF Cultural Center
The museum consists of offices, an exhibit gallery, archives, and a repository for the Nation. The Cultural Center provides a community space that includes offices, community classrooms, and an art studio. There is a large outdoor courtyard for cultural dancing and community events.
