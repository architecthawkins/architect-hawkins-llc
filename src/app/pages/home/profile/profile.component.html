Hello - I am Elizabeth Hawkins

I founded Architect Hawkins LLC with the mission of creating a design firm that provides quality design services in the commercial and residential sectors.

Design is about making decisions - lots of them, and at many different stages. Some decisions come easy, others require research and input. Such as in life, some decisions are small and can wait, and there are those that can not.

I help you navigate the process of conceptualizing a design idea and create sizable and measurable steps to move forward to realization.

Communication is open and honest.

I have been practicing architecture since 2004; I am a licensed architect in the State of Arizona. I hold an NCARB certification, providing the opportunity to work across the United States, through the ability of acquiring licensing reciprocity, through this certification. I have worked on multiple building types ranging from educational facilities, office buildings, support service facilities, as well as involved in residential work and consultation and planning and designing projects over the course of my experience.
